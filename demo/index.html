The Tribes of Eastern India

Odisha and Chatisgarh are Tribal dominated states in the eastern part of India. In Odisha, nearly 23% of the population belongs to tribal and indegenous groups. They have their own belief, unique living style and still belive in witch craft (black magic) and sorcery practices. Few of such tribes in Odisha includes:

  • Lanjia Souras
  • Dunguria
  • Bonda
  • Gadabas

Similarly, the neighbouring state of Chatisgarh include:

  • Maria
  • Baigas
  • Dhurubas (Gond Group)

They have their Ghotuls where young unmarries boys and girls meet in the evening for socialization like dancing, singing together and choose their partner.
